CU Overview

Chandigarh University is a full-fledged university established by the Punjab State Legislature and is recognized by University Grants Commission under Section 2(f) with the right to confer degrees as per Section 22(1) of the UGC Act, 1956.

Located in the vicinity of City Beautiful, Chandigarh University is a quintessence of exuberance of youth. The university cultivates a unique and inspirational academic model that focuses on the overall personality development of a student. Our renowned faculty further helps in the growth and assistance of curious minds, directing them on progressive career paths. Chandigarh University boasts of an unparalleled industrial patronage, thus exposing its students to emerging working methodologies. Consistent global exposure provides students with multiple options for an assured career in various professional spectrums.
About Chandigarh University:
The university enjoys cultural diversity with students from all states of India and various countries. With fourteen diverse educational setups, including programs in emerging fields like Agricultural Sciences, Business Analytics, Law and Philosophy, Chandigarh University has become an educational epitome of success in the region and has emerged to be the fastest growing private university in Asia.

CITY BEAUTIFUL – THE MOST PREFERRED DESTINATION

Chandigarh University is located in the vicinity of Chandigarh; a rare epitome of modernization co-existing with nature’s preservation. This city was envisaged by Late Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru and actualized by Le Corbusier. Serenity and a city are two diametrically opposite concepts, which however, get belied in the 'City Beautiful' The young-at-heart, fast growing and progressive city is stunning for its urban planning, impressive buildings with brise-soleil facades, well-organized sectors and broad tree-lined avenues.

THREE REASONS TO FALL IN LOVE WITH CHANDIGARH:

GLOBAL CITY :

Being the capital of 2 states, diverse cultures intermingle here to give Chandigarh a cosmopolitan outlook. Connected to the world through the international airport, the city has several bhavans, sabhas of different state governments, regional offices of various MNC’s and foreign embassies, promoting cultural diversity. Many artists and writers live in the city. The frequently held exhibitions at these bhavans and Tagore Theatre and performances by singers, dancers, musicians and actors have inculcated among city residents, a desire for the best in performing visual and plastic arts besides literary encounters.

HEART OF EDUCATION :

Chandigarh Education is informative, interesting and provides a complete and wholesome approach to learning. Comprising some of the finest institutes in North India, the student populace throngs here to pursue their education dreams. Chandigarh Administration envisages providing world-class education by developing an Entrepreneur Development Centre and Education City; fulfilling the requirement of training and educating young people in high end areas which are required in today’s context and also keeping in mind future projections.

LAND OF OPPORTUNITIES :

Chandigarh is an upcoming IT hub, offering promising growth in Hospitality, IT & Medical sector with presence of reputed companies, from around the globe. IBM, Infosys, Dell, Ranbaxy, Taj Group of Hotels, J.W. Marriott, Rajiv Gandhi IT Park, STPI and Fashion Technology Park offer numerous job prospects.
